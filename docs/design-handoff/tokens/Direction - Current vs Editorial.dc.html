The merge (4a)

No em dashes anywhere in app copy; periods and colons instead.

Why it doesn’t blend

What changed in 3b

One accent (blue, tappable things only). No gradients, no tinted fills, no pills, no card-in-card — sections divided by hairlines on the page surface. Chips became small-caps text labels. Claim bodies set in serif quotes: your past words read as quotation, chrome stays sans.

What it costs

Less scannable at a glance — the orange person chip was findable from across the room. Buttons as text links are quieter than 44px slabs (targets still 44px tall). And it drifts from stock-iOS toward Bear / iA Writer territory — a deliberate identity, but less “native.”

Middle path

Adopt 3b’s color discipline and serif claim voice, keep grouped cards and solid primary buttons from 3a. Say the word and I’ll mock it.
